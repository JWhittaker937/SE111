Review

Jared Whittaker08/13/2023 IMDB Starship Troopers

A young man growing up on earth finds his purpose on a whim when he decides to become a citizen.
In one of Robert A. Heinlein's most controversial books our young man Jaun Rico joins the military
as a member of the Terran Mobile Infantry. During his time in training he learns valuable skills as well as
helping him to become a strong and confident man. This sense of belonging and of personal strength resonates
with many men of all ages. While the story has been criticized and the government in the movie labeled as a
tyrannical fascist state the well thought out nature of the source material creates a wonderful parody.

The movie has become a cult classic with memes and references abound since its release. While I would much
prefer the book to the film they both serve a purpose of introducing the wild world of the not so distant future.
The special effects are awful. The writing layered on top of the book is cringe worthy and the blatant disregard for
sanity is hard to ignore. If however the viewer can forgo reality it can be a rather joyfilled film to get wasted to.

Triva

  • Nudity was removed in several versions of the movie.
  • The movie was intended to be satirical propaganda however it was not received as such by the public.
  • The US box office only grossed 54.7 Million
